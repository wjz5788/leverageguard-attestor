**Q: What permissions are needed?**  Only exchange read-only API, no trading permissions required.

**Q: Who pays the gas for payouts?**  The gas is paid by the wallet that initiates the claim, and the contract will transfer USDC to that wallet.

**Q: How to verify orders?**  Enter the order number on the claim page, the system will pull from the exchange and verify the fields before giving the result.
